Coas Records

Coas Records is a music label based in Johannesburg, South Africa that showcases the deeper, darker side of African electronic music.

Named after the ‘river of obscure water' that flows from Mount Kenya to the nourish parts of the Great Rift Valley, Ewaso Records will spread music to energize and inspire minds and movements across the world. 

Founded by Kamogelo Kgari & Henry Thage, both South Africans veterans in the ever evolving North Johannesburg music scene.
